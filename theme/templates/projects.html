PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Tryton’s project management solution enables team members to collaborate on projects and maintain accurate project status at all times, allowing admins to proactively identify and resolve potential issues with each task or ticket being created.

Keep Track of all Time Spent

Timesheet feature of Tryton project management app help managers to track the time spent by each user on a specific project and thus making the complete process efficient during invoicing and billing.

Easy task management

Tasks relevant to each project help you easily keep track of every job on a project, quickly telling you the who’s responsible, when it was due and why isn’t its completed!

KanBan and Calendar view

Work on tasks and issues using the kanban view and control deadlines in the calendar view. Drag and drop the tasks to respective stages as to notify the deadline.

Tags

Get organized as fast as you can think. Each task can be labeled with tags that make the complete process even more simpler. Easy categorization, great search capabilities makes the Tryton project management module a beast.

Smooth Workflow

From the inception of the project to its delivery, Tryton’s project management app is there to assist you at every step. You can record goals and milestones and map them against actual performance.
